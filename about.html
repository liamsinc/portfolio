About Me

Who Am I?

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Liam Sinclair and I'm 22 years old. I am a computer science graduate, having secured a 2:2 overall grade from the University of East Anglia. I am currently enrolled in the Netmatters Scion Scheme which builds upon the web development skills I learnt at university, teaches me about the best industry standards/practises while also improving my confidence as a developer. This is complimented by the constructive feedback I receive from a senior developer at Netmatters, which I firmly believe is the foundation upon which I can improve my coding skills.

My Education

I achieved four GCSEs in secondary school: English(B), Maths(5), Science(A), Computer Science (B)

I then spent two years at a sixth form college and completed an extended BTEC Diploma in IT, achieving the maximum grade of Triple Distinction *.

After being unconditionally accepted into the University of East Anglia, I spent 3 years studing computer science. I achieved the grades of second (first class) in my second year and second (second class) in my third year, ultimately leaving me with an overall grade of second (second class). I felt I had more potential, so I enrolled in the Netmatters Scion Scheme, to advance my knowledge and to prepare me for a real working environment. This additional education and experience has increased my technical competency and my understanding of how IT fits into a commercial environment.

My Hobbies

Despite being a very career-focused individual, I do have external hobbies and interests. I taught myself how to play the guitar roughly four years ago. I practise daily, and am constantly seeking to challenge myself through difficult songs. My favourite style of music is that which contains fingerpicking or arpeggiated chords.
