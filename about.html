About Me

Who Am I?

Hello and welcome to my website. My name is Liam Sinclair and I'm 22 years old. I am a computer science graduate, having secured a 2:2 overall grade from the University of East Anglia. I am currently enrolled in the Netmatters Scion Scheme which builds upon the web development skills I learnt at university, teaches me about the best industry standards/practises while also improving my confidence as a developer. This is complimented by the constructive feedback I receive from a senior developer at Netmatters, which I firmly believe is the foundation upon which I can improve my coding skills.

My Education

I achieved four GCSEs in secondary school: English(B), Maths(5), Science(A), Computer Science (B)

I then spent two years at a sixth form college and completed an extended BTEC Diploma in IT, achieving the maximum grade of Triple Dintinction *.

After being unconditionally accepted into the University of East Anglia, I spent 3 years studing computer science. I achieved a 2:1 grade in second year, and a 2:2 grade in third year, ultimately leaving me with an overall grade of 2:2. I was not happy with this grade considering the effort I had sustained throughout those three years and the fact that the pandemic significantly affected teaching quality/standards, and resulted in crucial feedback from module organisers becoming pretty much non-existent. This is partly the reason I applied for the Netmatters Scion Scheme, as it provides further training and the course structure helps prepare me for a real working environment. Additionally, it is something extra to add to my CV.
